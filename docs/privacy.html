Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

TrailPin does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your route data stays on your device.

Data Collection

TrailPin does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. We do not:

  • Collect personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Track your location beyond what is needed for route recording
  • Share any data with third parties
  • Use analytics or advertising SDKs
  • Require account creation

Data Storage

All route data, waypoints, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No data is ever sent to external servers.

Location Data

TrailPin uses your device's GPS to record routes. This data is used solely for displaying your route on the map and calculating statistics (distance, speed, elevation). Location data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted elsewhere.

Data Deletion

You can delete any recorded track at any time within the app. Uninstalling the app will remove all stored data.

In-App Purchases

Pro upgrade purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Purchase verification uses Apple's StoreKit framework.

Children's Privacy

TrailPin does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data, our app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
