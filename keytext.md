## Description (4000 char max)

TrailPin is the privacy-first GPS route tracker built for hikers, bikers, and walkers who want a simple, battery-efficient way to record their outdoor adventures.

NO ACCOUNTS. NO CLOUD. NO TRACKING. YOUR DATA STAYS ON YOUR DEVICE.

WHY TRAILPIN?

Unlike other GPS trackers that force you to create accounts, charge expensive subscriptions, or collect your data — TrailPin keeps everything on your device. No sign-up required. Just open and start recording.

CORE FEATURES (FREE)

• GPS Route Recording — Record hikes, bike rides, and walks with real-time distance, speed, and altitude tracking
• Live Map — See your route drawn on the map as you go
• Waypoints — Drop pins at trail junctions, viewpoints, and rest stops (up to 5 per track)
• Smart Battery Modes — Choose Performance (4-6h), Balanced (8-12h), or Ultra Saving (14-20h) to match your adventure length
• Auto Battery Optimization — Automatically switches to power-saving mode when battery is low
• Pause & Resume — Take breaks without splitting your track
• Track History — Browse your past adventures (up to 10 tracks)
• GPX Export — Share your routes with any mapping app
• Dark Mode — Easy on the eyes for early morning and late evening hikes
• Miles & Kilometers — Switch units anytime

PRO FEATURES ($3.99 ONE-TIME PURCHASE)

• Unlimited Waypoints — No 5-waypoint limit
• Unlimited Track History — No 10-track limit
• GPX Import — Load routes from other apps and follow along
• CSV Export — Export your data for analysis in spreadsheets
• Route Color Customization — Personalize your tracks
• Elevation Profile — Detailed altitude charts
• Speed & Altitude Charts — Analyze your performance
• Crash Auto-Recovery — Never lose a recording again

NO SUBSCRIPTIONS. EVER.

TrailPin is designed to last as long as your adventure:

• Ultra Saving Mode: Reduced updates, 14-20 hours of recording
